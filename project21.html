Information: We are interested in ways to get more middle school - high school students involved in parks and rec programming. Purpose: Right now my donors sometimes have to drive around to boxes if the first few are full. I appreciate their time and support, I would love for an app to easily direct them to what boxes are low and in need of supplies, without driving around town. It takes 3.5 hours to drive to each box in town, and fill it.
