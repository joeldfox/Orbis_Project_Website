Ankeny Parks & Recreation

Organization: Ankeny Parks & Recreation

Project Provider: Ryan Harkema

Contact Information: rharkema@ankenyiowa.gov

Information: We are interested in ways to get more middle school - high school students involved in parks and rec programming. From specific sports offerings, to direct programs like martial arts and cooking classes/computer classes, how do we get more involvement with this block of students? Looking at this from the side of how to market to the group on a continuous basis, to what types and prices of programming.

Purpose: This is a market outside of our niche, that we would love to be able to serve more often.
