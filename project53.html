Information: OVERVIEW The Ankeny School Foundation is a volunteer-run organization that raises money for grants to cover the cost of equipment and projects beyond the regular district budget. One of the foundation's major fundraising events is the Hawk Jag 5k Run. The event has minimal overhead and reliably turns a small profit. It has not, however, generated the number of participants or the margins expected for the amount of volunteer hours invested. Its future as an event is in question. GOALS 1. Orbis students will examine all aspects of the event and make recommendations on how to increase participation in the run. 2. Orbis students will examine all aspects of the event and make recommendations on how to increase revenue to $10,000. 3. Orbis students will provide three examples of other ways to raise money for the Ankeny School Foundation (game night, trivia night, BINGO night, etc.).

Purpose: 5/1/2023
