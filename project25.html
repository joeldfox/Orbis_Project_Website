Ankeny Police Department

Organization: Ankeny Police Department

Project Provider: Officer Walter Reed

Contact Information: WReed@AnkenyIowa.gov

Information: Police community engagement and the communities' involvement within the Police Department.

Purpose: The goal of this project would be to help the youth become more comfortable with the Police. The project would also bring the youth & community closer to the Police and aid in understanding the job of a Police Officer. The focus would be increasing Police community engagement and increasing the communities' involvement within the Police Department and developing ideas to better both.
