Project Provider: Terry Hatfield

Contact Information: dana@inspiringsolutions.com

Information: I would like students to make short videos to inform workers and employees about specific tools including safety and effective usage on construction sites. I would also like students to make short videos to inform workers and employees about OSHA safety videos instead of continuing to use boring paper and pencil.

Purpose: The goal of making these videos is to get the workers more engaged in the tool and OSHA safety videos. By watching these videos, it will improve the environment of our company by improving the safety of our construction processes.
