Contact Information: michael.mertz@ankenyschools.org

Information: I am interested in gather ideas on how to introduce DIY and woodworking to the younger generations. This could potentially start with High School/ MIddle School shop classes. Or... with the swing to more virtual classes, what would a virtual wood working class look like? All interesting opportunities to develop a base for the younger generation.

Purpose: The traditional woodworking demographic is getting older and Kreg is exploring ways to activate the next wave of wood workers. Introducing Kreg earlier in our customer' project building lifecycle will increase the life time value of that customer.
