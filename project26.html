Information: I am interested in gather ideas on how to introduce DIY and woodworking to the younger generations. This could potentially start with High School/ MIddle School shop classes. Purpose: The traditional woodworking demographic is getting older and Kreg is exploring ways to activate the next wave of wood workers. Introducing Kreg earlier in our customer' project building lifecycle will increase the life time value of that customer.
