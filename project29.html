Project Provider: Jared Masker

Contact Information: allen.raushel@kregtool.com

Information: I am looking for a team to model the existing Urbandale Food Pantry facility in order to maximize the space. This will include measuring and plotting out the space, using CAD to create a simple model, and identify potential efficiency improvements. ISG will provide guidance for the student led team.

Purpose: The Urbandale Food Pantry has reached the limits of it's current space, and this will help us identify potential efficiencies to continue to use the facility until a new one is procured.
